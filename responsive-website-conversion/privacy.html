Privacy Policy

Effective: January 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Aeroris AI. Your privacy is not just a regulatory requirement to us—it is a fundamental part of the systems we design. This policy outlines how we handle your data with transparency and care when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

We believe in minimal data collection. We only collect information that is absolutely necessary to provide you with our services, such as:

  • Identity Data: Name and job title (when you contact us).
  • Contact Data: Email address and phone number for communication.
  • Technical Data: IP address and browser type for security and analytics (anonymized where possible).

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data fuels the efficiency we promise, strictly for:

  • Delivering the automation services you requested.
  • Improving our internal systems and website performance.
  • Sending you relevant updates (only if you’ve opted in).

4. Data Sovereignty & Security

We implement enterprise-grade security protocols, including encryption in transit and at rest. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Ever. Access to your information is restricted to employees and contractors who have a legitimate business need.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or wish to exercise your data rights, please reach out to our privacy team directly at ai.aeroris@gmail.com.
